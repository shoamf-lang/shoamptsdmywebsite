Simple Breathing Exercise

  • Inhale normally and exhale slowly saying "calm" or "relax".
  • Long exhale reduces stress and tension.
  • Repeat as needed.

Sharing Your Story

Sharing your PTSD experience can be empowering, but do so carefully.

  • Reduces isolation
  • Organizes memories
  • Challenges negative beliefs
  • Helps others

Commit to Healing

  • Seek professional guidance
  • Build a support system
  • Practice self-care and coping strategies
