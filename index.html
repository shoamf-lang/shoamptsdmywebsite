Simple Breathing Exercise

  • Inhale normally, exhale slowly with a calming word (e.g., "calm").
  • Repeat several times to reduce stress and restore balance.

Sharing Your Story

Sharing your PTSD experience can be empowering and supportive. Consider:

  • Start with trusted individuals or professionals.
  • Share facts first, feelings later.
  • Use trigger warnings if posting publicly.
  • Focus on impact and growth rather than graphic events.

Committing to Healing

  • Seek professional guidance for trauma recovery.
  • Build a reliable support system.
  • Practice self-care, mindfulness, and grounding techniques.
  • Recognize that recovery is non-linear and setbacks are normal.
